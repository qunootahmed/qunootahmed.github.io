                      honoring tradition

                      Coffee Ceremony

                      Join us at our Renton Location where the ceremony begins with pan roasting green (unroasted) coffee beans, which are then brewed in a clay pot known as a Jebena. You will get to hear the origins of coffee, taste one of the oldest techniques for brewing coffee, and snack on traditional treats.
